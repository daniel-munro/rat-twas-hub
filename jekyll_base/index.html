--- title: Rat TWAS Hub ---

Candidate susceptibility genes for complex traits in outbred rats

This is a beta version. We are working on improving and expanding this portal.

TWAS (transcriptome-wide association study) is an approach to estimate the genetic association between disease risk and the local gene expression of thousands of genes. This web interface provides searchable access to TWAS results from complex traits and gene expression in Heterogeneous Stock rats. It is based on the original TWAS hub for human data.

What can you do here? Search the full list of analyzed traits and diseases in the TRAITS tab. See all genes and loci associated with a given trait like Velocity during novelty place preference test and browse cross-trait associations. See gene conditional analyses at individual loci like chr1:140,510,980-142,727,457 and filter all associated models. Evaluate predictive models for individual genes like Ctsc and browse all corresponding trait associations. All genes with predictive models are listed in the GENES tab. See USAGE for a more detailed guide.
